My Daily Routine

  1. Wake Up

    Get out of bed and freshen up to start the day.

  2. Exercise

    Do 30 minutes of stretching or yoga to stay fit.

  3. Breakfast

    Have a healthy breakfast with fruits and protein.

  4. Work/Study

    Focus on your professional or academic tasks for the day.

  5. Evening Walk

    Take a 20-minute walk to relax and clear your mind.

  6. Dinner

    Eat a light dinner and spend time with family.

  7. Sleep

    Go to bed early to ensure proper rest and recovery.
