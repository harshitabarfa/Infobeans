Chai: The Answer to Every Problem

A Cup of Comfort

Whether it's a breakup, a stressful exam, or a rainy day, chai has a magical way of making everything feel a little better. In many Indian households, the first response to tension or tiredness is, “Ek chai bana lete hain” (Let’s makesome tea).

More Than Just a Drink

Chai isn’t just a beverage—it’s therapy in a cup. Friends share it over deep conversations, coworkers take breaks with it, and families bond during evening tea-time. For every problem, big or small, chai is often the first step toward a solution.
